## At a glance
- **Role:** Skirmisher
- **Threat:** Low
- **Environment:** Underground
- **Signature:** Burrows to reposition and leaves heated ground behind.

## Tactics
- Start **burrowed** if possible; erupt behind the softest target.
- Hit once, then **burrow away** to deny opportunity attacks.
- If outnumbered, kite through tight tunnels to force single-file pursuit.

## Variants
- **Soot-Black Mole (stealth):** Gain proficiency in Stealth; lose Heated Burrow.
- **Cinder Mole (stronger):** +10 HP; Bite deals +2 fire damage on hit.

## Encounter hooks
- A mine’s support tunnels are collapsing—heated burrows have weakened the timbers.
- A smuggler ring uses ember moles to create “fresh” escape routes under a checkpoint.
