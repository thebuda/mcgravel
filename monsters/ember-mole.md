## At a glance
- **Role:** Skirmisher
- **Threat:** Low
- **Environment:** Underground, mines, volcanic tunnels
- **Signature:** Burrows to reposition and leaves heated ground behind.

## Tactics
- Start **burrowed** if possible; erupt behind the softest target.
- **Bite once**, then burrow away to deny opportunity attacks.
- In tight tunnels, use burrow movement to create **awkward angles** and force single-file pursuit.

## Variants
- **Soot-Black Mole (stealth):** Replace Perception +3 with Stealth +4; remove Heated Burrow.
- **Cinder Mole (stronger):** HP 28; Bite deals an extra 2 (1d4) fire damage.
- **Brood Swarm (hazard):** Two ember moles share a single initiative; if both are adjacent to the same target, that target has disadvantage on the first opportunity attack it makes each round.

## Encounter hooks
- A mine’s support tunnels are collapsing—heated burrows have weakened the timbers.
- Smugglers use ember moles to create “fresh” escape routes under a checkpoint.
- A volcanic shrine’s wards were undermined by tunneling; something worse is getting out.
